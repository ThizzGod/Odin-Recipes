Bangers and Mash

Description

A simple, classic, and delicious british dish; fried pork sausage over mashed potatoes all smothered in homemade onnion gravy. What a treat!

Ingredients

  • 4 links of pork sausage
  • 2 pounds of potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 2 large onions
  • 1 carton of beef broth
  • Butter, for cooking and buttering
  • Milk (optional) to taste for mashed potatoes
  • Dry mustard, salt, and black pepper to season the mashed potatoes

Steps

  1. Cook the sausages until brown.
  2. Boil, drain, mash, and season the potatoes.
  3. Cook the onions in butter, then add the broth and wine. Cook until reduced.
  4. Serve the sausages with the potatoes and pour the gravy on top.

And it's as easy as that! Enjoy!
